    Martin Garrix + x

    Martijn Gerard Garritsen born 14 May 1996), known professionally as Martin Garrix, is a Dutch DJ. He was ranked number one on DJ Mag's Top 100 DJs list for three consecutive years: 2016, 2017, and 2018. His most known singles are "Animals", "In the Name of Love", and "Scared to Be Lonely". He is also known by his pseudonyms "Ytram" and "GRX".
